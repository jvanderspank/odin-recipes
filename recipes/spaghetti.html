Spaghetti

Steps to make a really good Spaghetti!

  1. Make the sauce first
  2. Let it simmer for 3 hours
  3. Boil the fresh spaghetti noodles
  4. Assemble and drench in parmesan cheese

Ingredients you'll need

  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti noodles
